Shaded Relief 21 Day Bike Route

Our assignment for this map was to practice working with DEM raster data, hillshades, and raster landcover. After completing our basemap design of a region of our choice, we were then instructed to design a 21 day bike trip. I decided to design a route that I would consider taking someday. While it is not to be used for navigational purposes, I was happy to learn the workflow required for quality shaded relief styling, as well as day dreaming about a time in life where I could take this trip!
