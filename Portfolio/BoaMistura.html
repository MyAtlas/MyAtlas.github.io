Interactive Concept Design

This is another concept design completed for Cartography and Graphic Design at the University of Wisconsin Madison. Below is the interactive version and it was inspired by a team of artists out of Spain, Boa Mistura. It was done using Mapbox Studio Classic and then eventually it was briefly published on Carto. The labels are all done with the same typeface that many of the pieces they paint around the world are written in (Avant Garde Bold).
