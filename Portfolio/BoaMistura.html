Interactive Concept Design

This is another concept design completed for Cartography and Graphic Design at the University of Wisconsin Madison. Unfortunately, it is no longer active online, but at one point, it was an interactive map similar to my life map (see About Me page), and it was inspired by the artist collective out of Spain, Boa Mistura. It was done using Mapbox Studio Classic and then eventually it was briefly published on Carto.
