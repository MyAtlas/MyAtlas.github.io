Wisconsin State Cartographer's Office Public Land Survey System Application

While working for the Wisconsin State Cartographer's Office, one of my main responsibilities was maintaining the Public Land Survey System database that centralizes all Wisconsin County Land Survey data. The application (not my design), has since been redesigned and can be viewed by clicking on the image above. SQL, Python, ArcMap, Access, and PghAdmn were all parts of this workflow. Overall, this was my introduction to the development side of GIS, and needless to say, I'm hooked!
