ABOUT ME

My name is Dawson Bancroft-Short and the purpose of this website is to introduce you to the most recent life path that I have been focusing on and what I hope to be a lifelong career in GIS and cartography. I grew up flying planes with my grandfather and climbing mountains with my geologist grandmother, so maps have always played a large roll in my life. After high school, I went on to study Environmental Science at Chatham University in Pittsburgh, PA. I played hockey all four years, and for this reason, I have chosen hockey as my theme for my interactive CV map shown below. It was also the only time I saw a chance to be able to make a hockey map in my near future!




My time at the University of Wisconsin GIS certificate program has also connected many dots to my background in Environmental Science as well as how to apply them. I am a very open minded person who seeks to constantly keep learning new things and for that reason, I am open to any GIS or cartography opportunity that comes my way. I have a work ethic like an athlete and a mind like a scientist, so I look forward to seeing where I will end up applying these skills. I currently work for the Wisconsin State Cartographer's Office as a student GIS developer where I have worked with SQL, PostGreSQL, shell scripting, python, html, css, and javascript. I am not an expert in any of these, but I have a solid foundation laid for each.
